Kanji, 6-Stroke (Group 1)

“If you think you’re too small to have an impact, try going to bed with a mosquito in the room.” – Anita Roddick

Hey! Nice! You’re at 6-stroke kanji. That’s like… one whole stroke above where you wre just a moment ago :D

But seriously, 6-stroke kanji is great and is full of great kanji and words. Finally getting into some of the really common really useful kanji, I think, and it only gets better from here.

So, before you move on, make sure you go through the next 15 (yep, you’ve been upgraded to fifteen!) kanji – learn their meanings, on’yomi, and the “green” kanji vocab. You can do that right from here, or you can head over to the 6-stroke kanji page.

Sentence と Sentence と Sentence

“We all have the ability…we just don’t all have the courage to follow our dreams and to follow the signs.” – Paulo Coelho

Attempt to translate the following sentences to English. If you mess up on one, figure out what you did wrong, move on, then come back through again after you’ve finished.

小池さん　と　たべた

I ate with Koike

りんご　と　ぶどう　と　牛肉　を　かった

I bought apples and grapes and beef (and nothing else)

ボビーさん　と　いっしょ　に　えいが　を　み　に　いった

I went to go see a movie together with Bobby

中田さん　が　「おれ　は　かみさま　だ！」　と　言ったよ

Nakada said “I am God!”

「ゲーム・アブ・スローンズ」という　本　を　よんだ？

Did you read the book (known as) “Game Of Thrones”?

「ゲーム・アブ・スローんズ」という　テレビ　ばんぐみ　を　みた？

Did you see the television show (known as) “Game of Thrones”?

トマト　や　りんご　を　なげた

I threw a tomato and an apple (and some other things)

らいねん　日本　へ　いく　と　言いました

(He/She) said that he will go to Japan next year

わたし　は　ボビーさん　と　中田さん　と　いっしょ　に　テニス　を　する

I will play tennis together with Bobby and Nakada.

日本　に　いく　と　にほんご　を　ならう

If you go to Japan, you will learn Japanese

ステファニーさん　は　ボビーさん　と　いっしょ　に　フランスご　を　ならいたい　と　言った

Stephanie said she wants to learn French together with Bobby

If you don’t understand anything, go back and review this chapter (everything you need to know to know all these sentences should be here) and come back and try again. Once you’ve gone through all the sentences and understand them, you can move on!

By finishing this page, you’ve learned how to differentiate all the と grammar you’ve learned, and hopefully also got more comfortable with と overall

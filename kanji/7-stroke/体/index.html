体

Body

on’yomi kun’yomi Radicals
たい からだ ｲ + 本

Influential Person (ｲ) + Book (本)

Let’s use the kanji “book” instead of the radical “real” for this one. So, in this case, the leader is made of books!

He’s a scary thing. His BODY is entirely made of books.

Be sure to imagine the scene of this guy made of books. He’s terrifying – choose a size, maybe it’s super big, making it more terrifying. Scariest things is he’s influencing people to join his side (he is a leader, after all), even though you know he’s out there to kill us all with his BODY made of books.

Reading: たい

When we think たい we think of a “tie” (the one you put around your neck).

The only thing on this influential book-monster’s BODY is a TIE (たい). Other than that, he’s totally naked, with a you-know-what made of books as well.

You should look at this book-monster and think “isn’t it weird that he’s wearing a TIE and nothing else? Why a TIE of all things?”

Vocabulary

This is a pretty great set of words, especially the base word, 体.

a 体（からだ）＝　Body

  • Meaning: The same as the kanji.
  • Reading: The reading for this vocab is pretty long and doesn’t really sound like anything. So, try to memorize it now, then try recalling it in a few minutes (then an hour, and then so on) to build that memory up.

a 死体（したい）＝　Dead Body

  • Combo: 死 (death) + 体 (body)
  • Meaning: A body afflicted with death is a “dead body.”
  • Reading: The on’yomi readings of these kanji.

a 体力（たいりょく）＝　Physical Strength

  • Combo: 体 (body) + 力 (strength)
  • Meaning: Your body strength is your physical strength.
  • Reading: The on’yomi readings of these kanji.

a 体内（たいない）＝　Internal

  • Combo: 体 (body) + 内 (inside)
  • Meaning: Inside the body – that’s internal.
  • Reading: On’yomi readings of the kanji.

“It makes sense that no one else can cause you pain. That’s your job.” – Byron Katie

Time to learn the next set of kanji! Here they are for your learning pleasure. Just a reminder, this is where we’re starting to not worry about number of strokes anymore. We’ll be advancing up the stroke-levels quickly at this point, mixing kanji complexity in favor of how much you can use the kanji (basically, you’ll see some 7+ stroke kanji even though we’re on level 7 still).

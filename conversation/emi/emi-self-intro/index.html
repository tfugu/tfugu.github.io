1. Emi’s Self Introduction

“If your ship doesn’t come in, swim out to meet it.” – Jonathan Winters

In this lesson, Emi introduces and tells you a little about herself. She tells you about her hobbies, where she’s been, and what kind of person she is. After going through this lesson, you’ll be able to give your own self introduction, as well!

Line-By-Line Translation

aこんにちは、私の名前はエミです。
Hello/Good Afternoon, My name is Emi.

a私は今２３歳の独身です。
I am currently a 23 year old single person.

a趣味はランニング、ハイキング、サイクリング、映画鑑賞、ショッピングです。
My hobbies are running, hiking, cycling, watching movies, and shopping.

a私はとても明るく、いつも元気いっぱいです。
I am very cheerful and always full of energy.

a大学で中国語と経済を勉強しました。
In college, I studied Chinese and economics.

a今、ハワイに住んでいます。
Currently, I am living in Hawaii.

a私は旅行に行く事が大好きです。
I love to travel.

a今まで中国、韓国、マレーシア、フィリピン、台湾、香港などたくさんの国へ行ったことがあります。
Until now, I’ve been to many places such as China, Korea, Malaysia, the Philippines, Taiwan, and Hong Kong.

a私と友達になって下さい。
Please become friends with me.

aよろしくおねがいします！*
Nice to meet you!

Introducing Yourself

After you’ve gone through everything (and went through steps 1-10 in the Audio Lesson How-To), you should, in theory, be able to introduce yourself, even if it’s a slightly simpler introduction. Here’s a template you can use to get yourself started:

こんにちは、私の名前は＿＿＿＿＿です。

私は今＿＿歳の＿＿＿＿＿＿です。

趣味は＿＿＿＿、＿＿＿＿＿、＿＿＿＿＿、＿＿＿＿＿です。

私は＿＿＿＿＿＿＿です。

＿＿＿＿で、＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿を＿＿＿＿＿＿＿。

今、＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿＿に住んでいます。

私は＿＿＿＿＿事が大好きです。

今まで　[Something talking a little more about the above line]

よろしくおねがいします！

This template is slightly simplified (just a little bit), but you should try and use it to create your very own self introduction! Self introductions in Japan are surprisingly common, so it’s good to have something ready in your back pocket, should the situation ever arise!
